Q: Can I get same-day dental implants?
A: Yes! We offer immediate loading implants for suitable candidates. The implant is placed and a temporary crown is attached on the same day. Q: How long do dental implants last?
A: With proper care, dental implants can last 25+ years or even a lifetime. We use premium implant systems with proven 98%+ success rates. Regular dental hygiene, professional cleanings, and avoiding smoking are key to implant longevity. We provide lifetime warranty on the implant fixture itself.
Q: Is the procedure painful?
A: No! Implant placement is performed under local anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. Most patients report minimal discomfort afterward, which can be managed with over-the-counter pain medication. We also offer sedation options for anxious patients.
